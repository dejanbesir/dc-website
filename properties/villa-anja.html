Villa Anja

Sleeps 6 • 3 Bedrooms • Private Pool & Garden

From €450 / night

Villa Anja is a sun-drenched Dalmatian retreat perched on a secluded cove, blending modern lines with traditional stone architecture. Floor-to-ceiling windows frame uninterrupted Adriatic views, while your private infinity pool and landscaped terraced garden await just steps from the shore.

Inside, an open-plan living area, sleek kitchen, and bespoke furnishings create a relaxed yet refined atmosphere. Our dedicated local team is on call 24/7 to arrange private tours, island-hopping boat trips, and more.
